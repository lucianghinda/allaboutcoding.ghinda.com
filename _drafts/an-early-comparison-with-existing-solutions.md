On the product side, I would like to explore the existing solutions (or even the same) that are similar to what I want to build.

1. **Email Newsletters**: I am talking about the category of newsletters that do not focus on "news" or curate existing content but are more like sending good content via email with a periodicity - usually once per week. This is very similar to what I want to create here. The difference I want to bring is to group contents into topics so it will be easy to follow along with specific topics and offer the option to set the pace and choose when to receive the email. Another problem with existing solutions is that when someone *new* joins, they start receiving the latest emails but might miss a lot of great existing content.
    
2. **Drip Email Campaign**: Email platforms offer the option to have a drop email campaign. A drip campaign usually means that once someone subscribes to your email list, you can configure a series of emails to be sent to that subscriber with a pause between each email. With this feature, one can create an email course. What needs to be added from my perspective is allowing the subscriber to choose the frequency and when to receive the emails. To offer this way tools for the subscriber to control their learning experience.
    
3. **Courses platforms:** Some platforms for online learning offer the option to send the content added to the platform by email. This can range from simply sending new content by email to including some drip email features that allow a teacher/trainer to configure when and how to send the content via email. I think this could be an excellent choice for creating an email course, but I plan to focus on an email course and make the best experience out of it.
    

I am unsure if I analyzed all the options available as this is not market research. I just wanted to present roughly some thoughts about existing products or tools and what I think is missing for each. It is, of course, a subjective review where I look to lens them through th the solution I want to build.

I am pretty sure that this analysis is only helpful for me while thinking about my idea. It will probably not matter too much for someone looking to learn something new - what kind of product does the content creator use to deliver their knowledge?
